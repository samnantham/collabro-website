About Us

Collabro is a decentralised platform for entrepreneurs to collaborate, engage, find resources and do business. Built with AI-powered tripartite collaboration system technology to connect business owners and end-users. This platform will benefit those who are seeking resources to hire, buy, sell or offer services.
